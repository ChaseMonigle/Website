Fraternity

During my freshman year, I took a leap of faith and became a Founding Father of the Theta Xi chapter at Florida Tech, which started with only eight members. Over the years, I helped grow the fraternity into a 40+ member brotherhood, where I eventually served as Brotherhood Chair.

Through this role I developed valuable leadership, organizational, and interpersonal skills while building a chapter that would leave a lasting impact on campus. This past year, our fraternity achieved a major milestone by being officially chartered — and I had the honor of being featured on the front cover of Theta Xi’s national magazine.
